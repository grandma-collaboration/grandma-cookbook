Enrolling to Shifts#

Note: The starting of each [weekly slot will be during the GRANDMA telecon, on Thursdays at CET, and the week will have four slots of shifts per day - one will be taken by the weekly coordinator.]

So, 1 FA per shift plus the weekly coordinator. The slot of the weekly coordinator will be used for doubling up with someone new for training/support.

[The rota of the daily shift will be done at 00:00 CET, 06:00 CET, 12:00 CET, and 23:59 CET.]
